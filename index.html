About

The Devadan Cup 2024 is an exciting inter-college sports event that brings together students from various colleges to showcase their athletic prowess and sportsmanship. Organized annually, this event has become a highly anticipated competition that fosters a spirit of healthy competition and camaraderie among college athletes.

 

The Devadan Cup features a wide range of sports, including but not limited to football, basketball, volleyball, cricket, athletics, and more. The diverse array of sports allows participants to excel in their respective areas of expertise and promotes a holistic approach to physical fitness and well-being.

 

Organized by CHRIST(Deemed to be University), Bangalore
